One additional idea that adds real product value

Given that your project already stores medical reports, add a Medical Context Engine.

When generating recommendations, the engine should consider uploaded reports and diagnosed conditions. For example:

If the child has anemia, prioritize iron-rich foods and appropriate absorption pairings.
If the child has a kidney-related condition, avoid high-potassium or high-phosphorus suggestions when appropriate.
If growth parameters indicate undernutrition, prioritize energy- and protein-dense meal plans.

This makes the Nutrition Insights module use all the information parents already provided, creating recommendations that are more personalized and clinically relevant without requiring them to enter the same information twice.
